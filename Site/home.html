Fake News and Artificial Intelligence

Discover how misinformation affects society and how artificial intelligence can help fight it.

The impact of fake news

Fake news influences opinions, distorts information, and can cause serious social, political, and public health problems. It spreads quickly across social networks, confusing people and making informed decision-making harder.

What is Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is an area of computer science that creates systems capable of simulating human intelligence. It can learn patterns, analyze information, and make decisions based on data, making it a powerful tool in many fields.

Our AI approach against fake news

We developed an AI-inspired prototype capable of collecting similar news articles, analyzing their texts, and generating a similarity-based evaluation. This score helps highlight whether the content should be investigated further.
